# Privacy Policy for Prompt Compiler Interceptor

**Last Updated:** [Date]

This Privacy Policy describes how **Prompt Compiler Interceptor** ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our Chrome Extension.

## 1. Information We Collect
We collect the text you explicitly select or input into the extension's interface ("Prompts") for the sole purpose of processing and optimizing it via our backend service.

- **Prompts:** The text you wish to optimize.
- **API Keys:** Stored locally on your device (`chrome.storage.local`) to authenticate with our backend.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To provide the text optimization service.
- We do **not** sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties.

## 3. Data Retention
- Prompts are processed by our backend and returned. We do not permanently store your prompts unless explicitly stated for history features (which are stored locally or on your self-hosted backend).

## 4. Third-Party Services
- The extension communicates with a backend URL you provide (e.g., your Railway deployment or localhost). Please refer to the privacy policy of the backend service provider you are using.
